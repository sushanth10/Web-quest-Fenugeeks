About Us

With the support of the government of India's Make In India program led by honorable Prime Minister, Mr. Narendra Modi, we a small group of individuals look forward to bring traditional indian toys to international recognition. We are a passionate team of creative individuals who look forward to encourage the traditional indian toys in the Indian and international markets by bringing local vendors and manufacturers to the Chaturanga forum. Chaturanga already comprises of about 200 toy manufacturers across 7 indian states and we are working hard to bring their works to you.
